INSTRROL-2K24

Warm-Heartedly Invites you On behalf of ISoI Students Chapter

INSTRROL 2K24, organized by the ISoI Students' Chapter at KEC in collaboration with the Department of Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering, is set to be a grand affair that blends both technical and non-technical events. This annual symposium aims to create a dynamic platform where students can showcase their technical prowess and creative talents while fostering innovation and learning across multiple domains. Scheduled for March 14, 2024, INSTRROL 2K24 promises to be an exhilarating experience for participants and attendees alike.
